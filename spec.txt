[Some basic notes extracted from the spec]

Ingredients:
 @[^@]*{}
 @\w+
 Add @salt @ground black pepper{} to taste.

 qty in {}
 Poke holes in @potato{2}

 units add % between quantity and unit:
 Place @chopped spinach{1%bag} in pot
 and add @syrup{1/2%tbsp}

Steps: each blank-line-separated paragraph is a step,
adding a \ at the end includes the blank-line in the step:

  A step
  the same step

  a different step\
  with multiple lines

Comments:
  [x]  Inline comments from "--" to EOL
  [x]  Block comments from [- to -]

Metadata:
  [x]  YAML front-matter delineated by --- at the beginning of the file,
  ending with ---

    ---
  [x]title: Spaghetti Carbonara
  [x]author: Grandma
  [x]tags:
      - pasta
      - quick
      - comfort food
    ---

Cookware:
  #[^#]{}
  #\w+

Timer:
 anonymous: ~{25%minutes}
 named: ~eggs{3%minutes}

Notes:
  [x] > at the beginning of the line

Sections:
  [x] one more more "=" at the beginning of the line
  with optional trailing "="

  = Section

Include:
  [ ] like ingredients, but with relative path and without .cook extension:

  Pour @./saurces/hollandaise{150%g} over eggs
